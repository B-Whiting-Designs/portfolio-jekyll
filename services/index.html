A Method to My Madness

Identify Issues

Most designers want to rush into the project without finding out what the issue is at hand to begin with. Finding out the issue and coming up with a strategy to fix the problem is what has to be done first and foremost.

Strategize

Developing a strategy requires research and understanding of the issues identified at the start. Once I figure out that and make a plan it will be a full focus on getting a plan in order to make it happen.

Implement

Once we have the final draft done like a nicely made paper essay for your college credit, I will finalize it and get it ready for publishing. All the initial bells and whistles will be loaded for your site.

Releasing to You

Upon the release to you for the end result, all the tools used will be at your disposal - along with a full documentation on how to use if needed and a quick run down of any data to watch.
